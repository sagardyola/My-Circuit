About Us

MYCIRCUIT Pty Ltd is a family owned contract electronic manufacturer dedicated to provide a wide range of quality products and services throughout Australia. It is 100% Australian owned and operated.

We have a team of specialists who are highly skilled with over 15 years of experience. We prioritise quality and accuracy which meets enviable industry standards (IPC-A-610 Class 2 & Class 3).

Our Capabilities include

  • PCB Designing & Layout
  • Prototyping
  • PCB Assembly TH & SMT Components
  • Fine Pitch Reworks
  • Through Hole Assembly
  • Mechanical & Box Build Assembly
  • Cable and Harness Assembly
  • Production Testing & Quality Control
  • Component Procurement
  • Turnkey manufacturing

We value strong relationships with our customers and work closely with them to streamline their products to ensure ease of manufacture and to meet the requirement and budgets of small to medium sized customers. We keep our overhead and logistic costs to a minimum thus lowering production costs which gives us an advantage over competitors.

Our Goal is to offer world-class electronic manufacturing services and support.
